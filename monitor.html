What this monitor is

The PG27AQWP-W is ASUS's flagship 2025/26 esports OLED. The "-W" suffix is the White colour variant. It pairs a brand-new LG.Display 4th-gen Tandem WOLED panel with a 540 Hz refresh rate — one of the fastest gaming monitors on the market.

Headline features

⚡
540 Hz native QHD refresh, with a Dual-Mode 720 Hz at 1280×720 for competitive FPS.
🌟
1,500 nits peak HDR — the brightest OLED measured by TFTCentral (~1,801 nits in test).
🖤
TrueBlack Glossy coating, 1,500,000:1 contrast, 0.02 ms response.
🛡️
OLED Care Pro + Neo Proximity Sensor for burn-in protection; 3-year warranty (incl. burn-in, per ASUS global policy).

Who it's for

Competitive gamers and high-end creative users who want extreme motion clarity + OLED contrast. For an IT company this is a premium display — typically resold to gaming clients, creative studios, or used as a showcase/demo unit.

  • G-SYNC Compatible + AMD FreeSync Premium Pro
  • DisplayPort 2.1 (UHBR20, full 80 Gbps) + 2× HDMI 2.1
  • Fully ergonomic stand + 100×100 VESA mount

Reviews & known issues

👍 What reviewers love

  • RTINGS: "fantastic for PC gaming," "incredible for console gaming" — sharp motion at any refresh, very low input lag, near-infinite contrast.
  • TFTCentral: brightest OLED they've measured (~1,801 nits HDR peak); glossy coating gives excellent clarity.

👎 Watch-outs

  • Glossy screen → strong mirror-like reflections; needs controlled lighting.
  • 720 Hz Dual-Mode drops to 720p — "too drastic" for most uses.
  • RTINGS noted dark-gray uniformity issues; no Adobe RGB mode for pro print work.

Sources: RTINGS · TFTCentral
